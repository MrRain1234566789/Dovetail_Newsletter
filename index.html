We’ve got some exciting news: the Dovetail community is here!
It’s the first-ever marketplace for people to share and learn different approaches to uncover customer insights. Research findings can be hard to share, leaving many people who want to be closer to their customers wondering where to start.
Now you can get started quickly and confidently with 100+ project templates and tag boards from leading teams and your community. Choose a project template to suit your needs, add data, and start analyzing. Or add a tag board to any project to get a jump start on your tagging taxonomy.

Explore the community

That’s enough preamble. Now, let’s get to some of our favorites.
  1. Product feedback template: connect themes across NPS, in-product feedback, and more
  2. Kickstarter's customer engagement template: map the different stages of activation
  3. NPS response analysis template: use charts to turn your survey feedback into insights
  4. Maze's unmoderated usability testing template: use fields to slice up your data by browser, language, and more
  5. Creating personas tag board: differentiate the behavior patterns of users and non-users
  6. Sprig's CSAT tag board: group tags to identify areas of improvement, pains, and themes
The community isn’t just a place to explore and try templates. It’s an opportunity for you to contribute your own approach and elevate yourself and your team.
Explore, learn, and share in the new Dovetail community. Together, we’re building a community that’s contributing to the future of research—and we want you to be part of it!
